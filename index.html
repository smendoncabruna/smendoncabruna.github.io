Welcome to my (still under construction) website!

I am a brazilian Ph.D. student in University of São Paulo, but I am currently at the Center of Quantum Devices, in Denmark, as a guest researcher. My research is focused on the theoretical physics of topological materials and their ability of supporting robust quantum computation. Besides, I am part of a study group on Quantum Computation, where we have studied theoretical aspects, quantum algorithms on cloud quantum devices and we are currently working on optimizing a paper that used a quantum platform for modeling an Ising system.

My interest in quantum computation comes from noticing the fast pacing of the changes in paradigm of this area in both academia and industry. Quantum computers and quantum cryptography will, undoubtedly, have profound impacts on society. It appears that we are living in a very unique moment of history where we can make a difference by researching this area and thinking about the challenges that it will bring. I am excited to be a part of this!
